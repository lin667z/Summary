Chapter 14. Using the methods

“Success is neither magical nor mysterious. Success is a natural consequence of consistently applying basic fundamentals.”

~ Jim Rohn

 

Now that you know the fundamentals of the memory methods you can succeed with any information. By being more creative with information it helps increase your involvement with the content, and makes it part of your reality, therefore improving your memory. In this chapter I want to share with you how you can adapt the methods to remember almost anything. I will give you short guidelines on how to remember information word-for-word, remember presentations, how to get rid of your absent mindedness, remember playing cards, and how to remember anything that you choose to study.
