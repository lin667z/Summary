Take action now!

1. Identify your self-limiting beliefs.

2. Question these beliefs and ask yourself: “Is it I can’t improve my concentration and memory or is it I won’t make the time to improve my concentration and memory?”

3. What else do you believe about your mind and your potential?

4. Memorize this quote by Jim Rohn, “If you don’t like how things are, change it! You’re not a tree.”
